FAQ

The fine print… mostly painless.

How do I earn stamps?

Every drink purchase earns you one stamp. Just give your phone number at checkout — in-store or drive-thru — and we'll log it automatically.

What counts as a "treat"?

Your reward is a free drink up to a Lab (20 oz) size. Available across espresso, coffee, teas, Zoomies — whatever flavor your tail's wagging for.

Do I need an app?

Nope! Coffee Lab Rewards is powered by Toast, which lets us track everything by phone number — no app, no card, no extra anything.

Do stamps expire?

Stamps don't expire on a fixed schedule, so take your time. We'd love to see you sooner than later, though — the doggos miss you.

How do I opt out?

Reply STOP to any rewards text and you're out. You can also ask any team member in store and we'll take care of it.
