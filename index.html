Welcome to the Lab

Where every visit is a tail-wagging good time.

We feature a double drive-thru and an on-site dog park for our favorite furry friends. With a playful atmosphere, friendly staff, and a menu bursting with delicious beverages and convenient snacks, it's the perfect place to unwind and savor a delightful treat.

Our double drive-thru caters to those looking to grab a quick snack or refreshing drink without waiting in long lines or rustling the little ones from their back-seat slumbers. But if you need to stretch the legs and let the zoomies out, park up and enjoy our fenced-in dog run. While the doggos play, grab a treat from the seasonal walk-up window (March–November) and relax on one of two patios.

Like a dog at home wagging its tail for its owner, we're eagerly waiting and excited to meet and serve you. So next time you decide to put some pup in your cup, we'll be here, ready with impatient anticipation.

Until then — Woof Woof !!!
